How engagements are shaped

The engagement model is yours to choose. Fixed-scope projects, monthly retainers, quarterly agreements with named deliverables, and fractional capacity are all available. Trial engagements are common — most clients start with a defined scope and extend once the work proves itself.

The work is AI-native. AI accelerates the analytical and production work that traditionally requires a team. The senior operator does the judgment work that doesn't compress. This is the mechanism behind the speed, the lean staffing, and the cost difference from a traditional consulting structure.
